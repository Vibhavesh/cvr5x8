About CVR College of Engineering

CVR College of Engineering, established in 2001, is an esteemed engineering college dedicated to providing a holistic educational experience. Located in the heart of the city, our institution offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a vibrant learning environment that prepares students to face the challenges of the global engineering industry.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution in producing world-class engineers and leaders with a strong sense of responsibility, excellence, and innovation.
